    New Logo for CAL FIRE San Luis Obispo Unit, Division 6

    This is the new logo for the CAL FIRE SLU Pre-Fire Division (Division 6)

    The new logo has been utilized to mark maps and website products produced by the Pre-Fire Planning and GIS department, and it has been utilized for staff uniforms. Below is a sample proof for logo embroidered polo shirts and baseball caps.



    One Less Spark, One Less Wildfire Campaign

    This is a public banner for CAL FIRE San Luis Obispo's "One Less Spark, One Less Wildfire" campaign. The design was customized from graphic elements that were made available by the California Wildland Fire Coordinating Group (preventwildfireca.org).

    The banner is displayed on eight SLO County RTA buses during the years 2014 and 2015. The photo below is an example of one of the banners on a bus.



    2014 Chipping Event Voucher

    This is a voucher that was mailed out to residents living in rural, wooded regions in San Luis Obispo County, where CAL FIRE offered vegetation chipping events for the purpose of reducing fire fuels around homes. This voucher was a folded (quartered), double-sided print, complete with official information about how to create defensible space, instructions on how to prepare chipping piles, and a reservation form. Here is side 1...

    ...and here is side 2. The far right panel is the form which residents could fill out and mail in to reserve their spot to have their vegetation pile chipped during the event
