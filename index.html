One Less Spark, One Less Wildfire Campaign

This is a public banner for CAL FIRE San Luis Obispo's "One Less Spark, One Less Wildfire" campaign in the year 2014. The design was customized from graphic elements that were made available by the California Wildland Fire Coordinating Group (preventwildfireca.org).

The banner is displayed on eight SLO County RTA buses. The photo below is an example of one of the banners on a bus. The banner measures 26"x120".



Ready, Set, Go! Campaign

New for the year 2015, these are public banners for CAL FIRE San Luis Obispo's "Ready, Set, Go!" campaign. Based on the CAL FIRE's "Wildfire is coming. Are you ready?" campaign (readyforwildfire.org), the customized graphic elements were made available by the California Wildland Fire Coordinating Group (preventwildfireca.org). This year, there will be two different sizes displayed. One will be 26"x120" (top image), while the other one will be 26"X84" (bottom image).
